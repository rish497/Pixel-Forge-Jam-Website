About

Pixel Forge is a 1-week game development hackathon where developers, artists, designers, and creators come together to build something awesome. You have 7 days to create a game from scratch, experiment with ideas, and turn creativity into playable projects. Whether you're a beginner or experienced developer, Pixel Forge is all about creativity, learning, and building cool games. Not only will you have a shiny new game to add to your portfolio, but you'll also receive recognition, feedback, and the chance to win prizes!

Don't worry if you can't participate in this jam, as we are running more iterations in the future!

Rules

1. Submissions

Your game must have been entirely made during the jam period. You may use small pre-existing assets and tools, but disclose and properly credit them in your game description. You should also include an explanation of how your game relates to one of the themes. You can use any game engine!

2. Progress

YOU MUST KEEP TRACK OF YOUR PROGRESS! This can be done either by taking screenshots every day with the date/time visible, or by having a public GitHub repository for your project. We may ask to see it at the end of the jam.

3. Team Size

Solo developers and teams of up to 4 people are allowed. Join our Discord server to easily find teammates!

4. Game Content

Games should generally be appropriate for all ages, with the exception of scary/violent content in horror games. No offensive, hateful, or explicit content.

5. Be Kind

Provide constructive feedback to others and maintain a supportive community atmosphere!

Q & A

Question: What is the theme?

Answer: The theme will be announced when the jam begins.

Question: Does my game have to be a certain format?

Answer: No, but remember that not all users have Windows/Mac/Linux devices. Browser based is a good idea to get as many people playing your game as possible and try to make at least a Windows version.

Question: What assets can I use?

Answer: The vast majority of code must be written during the jam, but you can use whatever art and audio assets you have the legal right to use.

Question: Can I submit a game that I already started on?

Answer: No, it is required that all participants make the entirety of the game in the time provided.

Question: Is there a Discord I can join?

Answer: Yes, click here to join.
