Pixel Forge Jam #2 [HAUNTED EDITION]

After an incredible first jam, we are officially getting ready for our next one.

By Rishabh Mittal 27 June 2026 4 min read

When we started Pixel Forge Jam, our goal was simple: create a space where developers, artists, designers, and beginners could come together, make games, learn new skills, and share their work with the world.

We did not expect our first jam to grow the way it did. Pixel Forge Jam 2026 brought in over 500 participants, 81 submissions, more than 11,000 views, a prize pool worth over $4,000, and a Discord community of more than 350 members. Seeing so many people join, build, submit, rate games, and support each other was honestly amazing.

The best part was not just the numbers. It was seeing people finish real games, try new ideas, collaborate with others, and create projects they could be proud of. Some people joined with experience, while others were trying game development for the first time. That is exactly what Pixel Forge Jam is supposed to be about.

Announcing Pixel Forge Jam #2

We are excited to announce that Pixel Forge Jam #2 will run from June 27 to July 4, 2026. It will once again be an online game jam where participants will have one week to create a game based on a theme revealed at the start of the event.

Just like the first jam, this event is focused on creativity, experimentation, and learning. Whether you are a beginner making your first game or an experienced developer looking to challenge yourself, Pixel Forge Jam #2 is built to give you a reason to start, finish, and share something real.

Why We Are Doing This Again

The first Pixel Forge Jam showed us how much potential this community has. People did not just submit games; they gave feedback, joined discussions, supported other creators, and helped make the jam feel alive.

That success made us even more excited to continue building Pixel Forge. We want Jam #2 to be bigger, smoother, and even more fun. Our goal is to give participants a better experience, more opportunities, more community interaction, and another chance to create games they can showcase proudly.

Pixel Forge Jam #2 is not just about making a game. It is about learning, experimenting, finishing something, and being part of a creative community.

What to Expect

Pixel Forge Jam #2 will follow the same core idea: build a game in a limited time span, submit it, play other entries, and be part of the community voting process.

  • Dates: June 27 to July 4, 2026
  • Format: Online
  • Duration: 7 days
  • Submission platform: itch.io
  • Teams: Solo or teams of up to 4 people
  • Theme: Revealed when the jam begins
  • Community: Discord based

We are also working on bringing in sponsors, prizes, and community opportunities that make the event more exciting for everyone involved. The first jam had a prize pool worth over $4,000, and we are hoping to continue giving participants meaningful rewards, recognition, and reasons to push their ideas further.

For New Participants

If you missed the first jam, this is a great time to join. You do not need to be an expert to participate. Pixel Forge Jam is meant to be welcoming to beginners while still giving experienced developers a challenge.

You can use any game engine or tool you like. You can work alone or with a team. The most important thing is that you make something during the jam, connect it to the theme, and submit it before the deadline.

Thank You to Our Community

Thank you to everyone who joined the first Pixel Forge Jam, submitted a game, rated entries, gave feedback, joined the Discord, sponsored the event, or helped spread the word. The first jam was successful because of the community around it.

We are incredibly excited for Pixel Forge Jam #2, and we cannot wait to see what everyone creates next.

Get ready. The forge opens again on June 27, 2026.
