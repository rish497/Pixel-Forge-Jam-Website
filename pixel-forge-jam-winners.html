Congratulations to the Pixel Forge Jam Winners

Our first Pixel Forge Jam is officially complete, and we are so excited to celebrate the amazing games our community created.

By Pixel Forge Team 27 June 2026 4 min read

Pixel Forge Jam started with a simple goal: bring creators together, give them a theme, and challenge them to build something real in a short amount of time.

Our first jam completely exceeded our expectations. We had over 500 participants, 81 submissions, more than 900 ratings, and over $4000 in prizes. More importantly, we got to see so many people create, experiment, collaborate, and finish games they can be proud of.

Thank you so much to everyone who participated, submitted a game, rated entries, gave feedback, joined the Discord, or supported the jam in any way. The quality of the submissions genuinely surprised us, and choosing the winners was not easy.

Our Top Winners

A huge congratulations to our top five winners. These games stood out during the jam, and we highly encourage everyone to check them out, play them, and support the creators.

Congratulations to Tic Tac Toe Maybe for taking first place in Pixel Forge Jam. This game earned the top spot, and we are so excited to feature it as our first-place winner.

Thank You to Every Participant

Even if your game is not listed here, we want to say this clearly: thank you. Every submission helped make the first Pixel Forge Jam special. The jam would not have been possible without the people who took the time to create something, submit it, rate other entries, and support the community.

Game jams are not only about winning. They are about learning, finishing something, trying risky ideas, and sharing your work with others. We are proud of every creator who joined us for this first event.
